Our Story

We specialise in launch activations, social events, virtual reality, employee engagement, training and consulting among other things. We strive to make every event full of joy and excitement for you and your guests with our experienced team and commitment to deliver the best!

This passion for delivering the best experiences comes from our founder, Megha. A pioneer in event management, she boasts an experience of 17+ years in the corporate arena before realising people and events are what she loves the most, leading to the inception of Harmonyinlife with the values of love for people, joy, passion and excellence.
